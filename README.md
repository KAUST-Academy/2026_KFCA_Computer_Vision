# King Fahd Causeway Authority Training Program
## Computer Vision — Week 1: Foundations, Detection & Segmentation
*Designed by the KAUST Academy*

A static course website with lectures and labs for Week 1, organised into one
page per day. Each day links to its lecture slides (`Slides/Day_X/`) and to its
labs and homework notebooks (`Labs/Day_X/`), which open directly in Google Colab.

---

### Day 1 — Foundations, Classical CV & CNNs
Overview of computer vision, classical techniques, and CNN foundations. PyTorch
basics, data augmentation, dataset structures, custom `Dataset` class, CNN from
scratch (Fashion-MNIST), CIFAR-10 classifier, and a CNN classification homework.

### Day 2 — Modern CNN & Transformer Architectures
Modern CNN architectures and transformer-based vision (ViT, Swin). VGG transfer
learning, fine-tuning (Cats & Dogs), EfficientNetV2 image search, ViT
fine-tuning, and a ViT/Swin cell-classification homework.

### Day 3 — Object Detection
Key methods, architectures, and frameworks (R-CNN & YOLO families). Faster R-CNN,
YOLO, and Faster R-CNN / Oriented YOLO homeworks.

### Day 4 — Semantic & Instance Segmentation
Semantic and instance segmentation (Mask R-CNN and related models). Custom U-Net,
SMP U-Net, brain MRI tumor segmentation, YOLO fire & smoke segmentation, and an
optional PointNet 3D notebook.

### Day 5 — Learning from Videos
Video understanding, action recognition, and temporal analysis. Video CNN and a
video-classification fine-tuning homework.
